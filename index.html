South Dartmoor Social Cricket League

Teams from: Ashburton, Buckfastleigh, Ilsington,
Manaton and Moretonhampstead, and South Brent.

Welcome to the website of the South Dartmoor Social Cricket League. 2022 is the first season five teams from the southern side of Dartmoor have grouped together to play an informal 20-20 cricket league. This website will keep you up to date with the fixtures, results and league table as the season progresses.
